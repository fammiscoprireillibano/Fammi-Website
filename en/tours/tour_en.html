Lebanon: A Mosaic of Millennia-Old Civilizations

Lebanon is one of the richest countries in Mediterranean history. Within just a few kilometers, you encounter ancient civilizations, religious traditions, breathtaking landscapes, and cultures that have left a profound mark on human history.

Phoenicians, Greeks, Romans, Byzantines, Arabs, Ottomans, and both Christian and Muslim communities have lived, built, and preserved a unique heritage of monasteries, temples, port cities, sacred valleys, and pilgrimage sites.

Our daily tours are designed to guide you through this extraordinary heritage with authentic experiences, far from mass tourism. Each proposal is tailor-made, paced gently and thoughtfully, allowing you to experience every place in a meaningful and intimate way.
